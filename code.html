About us

We like cars. Small ones. Fast ones. Ones that can jump flaming loops, fly off rooftops, or launch across your living room like a miniature missile.

We're the kind of people who think orange track is basically interior design. We've eaten cereal while building mega-loops. We've raced cars on treadmills. We've duct-taped tracks to the ceiling. And no, we’re not sorry.

Our dream garage has 200 cars, 0 responsibilities, and one absolutely unreasonable jump ramp powered by imagination and maybe a leaf blower.
